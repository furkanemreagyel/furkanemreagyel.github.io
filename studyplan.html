Furkan's Study Plan 2025

Each Day is collapsible. Under each Day, you have four subtitles: Physics 2, Circuit Theory, MA 2, and LAG (unless that day has none or “No tasks”).
• Physics 2 contains your “Lecture X,” “Exercise X,” “Recap” tasks.
• Circuit Theory contains all slides (00–15c) and circuit references.
• MA 2 has the tasks from your plan (theory, exercises, Moodle, etc.).
• LAG has notes, textbooks, or general review tasks.

You can check tasks, add or remove them, or add/remove entire subtitles.
